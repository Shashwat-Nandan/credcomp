## Editor's Take

The HDFC Infinia is widely regarded as one of India's best credit cards for frequent travellers and high spenders. With an uncapped 3.3% reward rate on all spending and the ability to transfer points to over 10 airline and hotel loyalty programmes, it offers unmatched flexibility.

### Who Should Get This Card?

The Infinia is an **invite-only** super-premium card, so you'll need an existing relationship with HDFC Bank and significant spending history. If you spend over ₹10 lakh annually on travel, dining, and lifestyle, the value far outweighs the annual fee.

### Reward Maximisation Tips

- **Transfer to Marriott Bonvoy** for the best hotel redemptions — you can get 5-star stays worth ₹20,000+ for just 30,000 Infinia points.
- **Use SmartBuy** for flight and hotel bookings to earn 10x accelerated rewards on top of base earn rate.
- **International spending** is lucrative despite the 2% forex markup — the 3.3% reward rate gives you a net positive.

### The Bottom Line

If you can get your hands on the Infinia, it's a no-brainer for anyone who values travel rewards and premium experiences. The combination of uncapped rewards, comprehensive lounge access, and strong insurance coverage makes it the gold standard in India's credit card market.
