## Editor's Take

The SBI Elite is a solid mid-premium card that punches above its weight in the lounge access department. With complimentary Priority Pass membership and strong milestone benefits, it's a practical choice for frequent flyers on SBI Card's network.

### Who Should Get This Card?

If you're an **SBI banking customer** who travels domestically 5+ times a year, the Elite makes financial sense. The annual fee is waivable at ₹10 lakh spend, which is reasonable for a premium card.

### Reward Maximisation Tips

- **Use the dining 10x accelerator** to earn bonus points at partner restaurants — this is one of the highest dining earn rates in the market.
- **Hit the ₹10 lakh milestone** to get the annual fee waived and bonus reward points.
- **Redeem points via SBI Card Reward Store** for best value — avoid cash redemption as the value drops significantly.

### The Bottom Line

The SBI Elite is a dependable, no-fuss premium card. It may not have the highest earn rates or the most glamorous benefits, but it delivers consistent value across travel, dining, and lifestyle categories. Highly recommended for SBI customers looking to upgrade from entry-level cards.
